# Beta Trial Packs

`vibe beta-trial create` writes a local beta trial guide, checklist, feedback template, incident template, and summary under `.vibecli/beta/trials`.

Commands:

- `vibe beta-trial create`
- `vibe beta-trial create --target solo-developer`
- `vibe beta-trial list`
- `vibe beta-trial show <trial-id>`

Trial packs are local files only. They are not uploaded or emailed.
