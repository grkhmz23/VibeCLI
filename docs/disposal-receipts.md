# Disposal Receipts

Exact-confirmed local disposal writes:

- `DISPOSAL_RECEIPT.json`
- `DISPOSAL_RECEIPT.md`
- `DELETED_ARTIFACTS.json`
- `RECOVERY_GUIDANCE.md`

Receipts record pre-delete SHA-256 hashes, deleted paths, skipped files, failures, legal hold state, archive verification state, and the retention-ledger event hash when available.

Receipts are local evidence only. They are not immutable proof, legal advice, or certified retention compliance.
